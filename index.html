I'm currently a PhD student in Geometric Analysis at University College London supervised by Costante Bellettini. My research is focused on singular minimal hyper-surfaces, specifically those produced by min-max methods. Broadly, my mathematical interests are in the areas of differential geometry, geometric measure theory and partial differential equations. I completed my undergraduate and master's at The University of Warwick.
