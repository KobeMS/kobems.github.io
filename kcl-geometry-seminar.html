KCL Geometry Seminar

The King's College London geometry seminar runs weekly during term time in-person. Currently no hybrid option is offered. Talks take one hour, directly after we go for a coffee break in the common room. The seminar is organised by Daniel Platt. If you want to receive regular seminar announcements, please email me at daniel.platt.berlin@gmail.com and I will add you to the mailing list.
